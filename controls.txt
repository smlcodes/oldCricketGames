Batting: (assumes right-handed batsman)

Moving: (can only move when not charging a shot)

A - move left
S - move down
D - move right
W - move up

Front-foot (O):

A - square drive
Z - cover drive
X - straight drive
C - on drive
D - pull
E - sweep
Q - late cut
S - defensive

Back-foot (P):

A - cut
Z - backfoot cover drive
X - backfoot straight drive
C - backfoot on drive
D - hook
E - leg glance
Q - upper cut
S - backfoot defensive

hold O/P and a shot to charge power and release the buttons to play the shot

Bowling:

O - selects delivery type & marker
hold O when running in to charge speed
hold O when delivering to charge amount of swing/seam/spin
